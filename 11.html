Matt 19:3-6GOSPEL

 At that time: The Pharisees came to him, and to test him they asked, ‘Is it lawful for a man to divorce his wife for any cause?’ He answered, ‘Have you not read that the one who made them at the beginning “made them male and female”, and said, “For this reason a man shall leave his father and mother and be joined to his wife, and the two shall become one flesh”? So they are no longer two, but one flesh. Therefore what God has joined together, let no one separate.'

After the Gospel the faithful respond: 

 M. Laus tibi, Christe.
 W. Praise be to Thee, O Christ.

Mass of the Faithful

Offertory

The priest kisses the altar and greets the faithful, inviting all to prayer. 

 S. Dóminus vobíscum.
 M. Et cum spíritu tuo.
 S. Orémus.
 K. The Lord be with you.
 W. And with thy spirit.
 K. Let us pray.

The priest performs the rites of offering. During this time, the choir sings the offertory antiphon of the day. Meanwhile, the priest recites it himself. In the moment of offering, let us give to the Lord Jesus our whole life, all our deeds, worries, and joys; let Him place these gifts of ours before the Throne of God in heaven. At the end, the priest silently recites the prayer called the secret. 

Ps 30:15-16OFFERTORY ANTIPHON

 In te speravi, Domine: dixi: Tu es Deus meus: in manibus tuis tempora mea.
 In Thee, O Lord, have I hoped: I said, Thou art my God, my times are in Thy hands.

SECRET

 Suscipe, quaesumus, Domine, pro sacra connubii lege munus oblatum: et, cuius largitor es operis, esto dispositor. Per Dominum.
 Receive, we beseech you, O Lord, graciously accept this offering made for the sacred law of marriage; and as you are the author of the work, so be its dispenser. Through our Lord.

Then the priest says aloud:

 S. Per omnia sæcula sæculorum.
 M. Amen.
 K. World without end.
 W. Amen.

Preface

 S. Dóminus vobíscum.
 M. Et cum spíritu tuo.
 S. Sursum corda.
 M. Habémus ad Dóminum.
 S. Grátias agámus Dómino Deo nostro.
 M. Dignum et iustum est.
 K. The Lord be with you.
 W. And with thy spirit.
 K. Lift up your hearts.
 W. We have lifted them up to the Lord.
 K. Let us give thanks to the Lord our God.
 W. It is meet and just.
