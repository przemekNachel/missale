The Nuptial Blessing

After the Pater Noster, the newlyweds ascend the steps of the altar and kneel. The priest recites the following prayers over them:

PRAYER

 Be attentive, O Lord, to our supplications, and graciously assist the institutions which Thou hast ordained for the propagation of the human race, so that the union which is made by Thy authority may be preserved by Thy help. Through our Lord.
 Propitiare, Domine, supplicationibus nostris, et institutis tuis, quibus propagationem humani generis ordinasti, benignus assiste: ut, quod te auctore iungitur, te auxiliante servetur. Per Dominum.

PRAYER

 O God, who by Thy mighty power didst make all things out of nothing, who, having set in order the beginnings of the universe, didst create for man, made to the image of God, the inseparable help of woman, fashioning her body from the flesh of man, to teach us that what it pleased Thee to form from one substance it should never be lawful to put asunder. O God, who by so excellent a mystery hast consecrated the union of the married state, that Thou didst foreshadow in the nuptial contract the mystical union of Christ and His Church.
 O God, by whom woman is joined to man, and the partnership ordained from the beginning is endowed with such a blessing that it alone was not taken away either by the punishment for original sin or by the sentence of the flood.
 Look down propitiously upon this Thy handmaid, who, being now to be joined in the bond of marriage, seeks to be safeguarded by Thy protection. May it be to her a yoke of love and of peace; faithful and chaste, may she marry in Christ, and may she ever remain a follower of holy women.
 Deus, qui potestate virtutis tuae de nihilo cuncta fecisti, qui dispositis universitatis exordiis, homini, ad imaginem Dei facto, ideo inseparabile mulieris adiutorium condidisti, ut feminco corpori de virili dares carne principium, docens, quod ex uno placuisset institui, numquam licere disiungi, Deus, qui tam excellenti mysterio coniugalem copulam consecrasti, ut Christi et Ecclesiae sacramentum praesignares in foedere nuptiarum.

 Deus, perquem mulier iungitur viro, et societas principaliter ordinata, ca benedictione donatur, quae sola nec per originalis peccati poenam nec per diluvii est ablata sententiam.

 Respice propitius super hanc famulam tuam, quae, maritali iungenda consortio, tua se expetit protectione muniri, sit in ca iugum dilectionis et pacis, fidelis et casta nubat in Christo, imitatrixque sanctarum permaneat feminarum.
