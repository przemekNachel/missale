The priest blesses the rings, singing in Latin:
 V. Our help is in the name of the Lord.
 R. Who made heaven and earth.
 V. O Lord, hear my prayer.
 R. And let my cry come unto Thee.
 V. The Lord be with you.
 R. And with thy spirit.
 V. Adiutórium nostrum in nómine Dómini.
 R. Qui fecit caelum et terram.
 V. Dómine, exáudi oratiónem meam.
 R. Et clamor meus ad te véniat.

 V. Dóminus vobíscum.
 R. Et cum spíritu tuo.
Prayer  
  Let us pray.

O Creator and Preserver of the human race, Giver of spiritual grace and Author of eternal salvation, we beseech Thee: vouchsafe to bless these rings, which we bless in Thy holy name. May those who wear them abide in Thy peace, remain in Thy will, live and grow in Thy love, and reach an advanced age, and through a numerous offspring may they reach into the far future. Through Christ our Lord.
 R. Amen.
  Oremus.

Creátor et conservátor géneris humáni, dator grátiae spirituális, cónditor salútis aetérnae: quaesumus, bene dícere dignéris hos ánulos, quos nos in nómine tuo sancto benedícimus; ut qui eos portáverint, in tua pace consístant, in tua voluntáte permáneant, et in tuo amóre vivant, crescant et senéscant atque multiplicéntur in longitúdinem diérum. Per Christum, Dóminum nostrum.


 R. Amen.

The Examination

The priest asks the bridegroom:N., do you take N., here present, for your lawful wife according to the rite of our holy Mother the Church?
He answers:I do.
The priest:Are you willing to persevere in this union in good times and in bad, until death does you part?
He answers:I am.
The priest:Are you willing to accept and raise in the Catholic faith the children with whom God may bless you?
He answers:I am.
Then the priest asks the bride:N., do you take N., here present, for your lawful husband according to the rite of our holy Mother the Church?
She answers:I do.
The priest:Are you willing to persevere in this union in good times and in bad, until death does you part?
She answers:I am.
The priest:Are you willing to accept and raise in the Catholic faith the children with whom God may bless you?
She answers:I am.
