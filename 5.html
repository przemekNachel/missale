Priest: 

 May Almighty God grant you His grace, that what you shall speak may be the rule of your life in marriage.

The Marriage Contract

The Priest says: 

 Join your right hands.

He then wraps the right end of his stole around the joined hands of the spouses in the form of a cross and bids them speak the words by which the marriage is completed, saying: 

 In order to enter into the holy bond of Matrimony, repeat after me the words of the oath.

The bridegroom repeats after the priest: 

I, N., TAKE YOU, N., FOR MY LAWFUL WIFE
AND I PLEDGE TO YOU LOVE, FAITHFULNESS
AND MARITAL HONESTY,
AND THAT I WILL NOT ABANDON YOU UNTIL DEATH.
SO HELP ME,
ALMIGHTY GOD,
ONE IN THE HOLY TRINITY, AND ALL THE SAINTS.

Then the bride repeats after the priest: 

I, N., TAKE YOU, N., FOR MY LAWFUL HUSBAND
AND I PLEDGE TO YOU LOVE, FAITHFULNESS
AND MARITAL HONESTY,
AND THAT I WILL NOT ABANDON YOU UNTIL DEATH.
SO HELP ME,
ALMIGHTY GOD,
ONE IN THE HOLY TRINITY, AND ALL THE SAINTS.

wwExchange of the Rings

The priest removes the stole and says to the new husband: 

  N., as a sign of the marriage contracted, place the ring upon your wife's finger and repeat after me.

The husband takes the ring from the priest and places it on his wife’s finger, saying: 

  Wife, receive this ring, a sign of matrimonial fidelity, in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Then the priest turns to the wife and says: 

  N., as a sign of the marriage contracted, place the ring upon your husband's finger and repeat after me.

The wife takes the ring from the priest and places it on her husband’s finger, saying: 

  Husband, receive this ring, a sign of matrimonial fidelity, in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.
