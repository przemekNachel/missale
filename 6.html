Exchange of Rings

The priest removes the stole and says to the newlywed: 

  N., as a sign of the marriage you have contracted, place the ring on your wife’s finger and repeat after me.

The husband takes the ring from the priest and places it on his wife’s finger, saying: 

  My wife, receive this ring as a sign of marital fidelity, in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

Then the priest turns to the wife and says: 

  N., as a sign of the marriage you have contracted, place the ring on your husband’s finger and repeat after me.

The wife takes the ring from the priest and places it on her husband’s finger, saying: 

  My husband, receive this ring as a sign of marital fidelity, in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.

The priest concludes with a blessing: 

  May these rings be for you a pledge of heavenly blessing and a help in attaining eternal salvation.

Confirmation of Marriage

The priest says: 

  “What God has joined together, let no man separate” (Mt 19:6). The marriage you have contracted between yourselves I confirm and bless by the authority of the Catholic Church, in the name of the Father and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit.
  R. Amen.

CALL OF THE FAITHFUL TO PRAYER

The priest addresses those present: 

  I call upon all present here as witnesses, so that if necessary they may testify to this marriage, contracted before God and confirmed by the Church.

  Let us pray, brothers and sisters, for God’s blessing upon the newlyweds, that they may faithfully fulfill what they have vowed.
