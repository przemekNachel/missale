The Confirmation of the Marriage

The Priest says: 

  “What God has joined together, let no man separate” (Matt 19:6). The marriage which you have contracted between yourselves, I, by the authority of the Catholic Church, confirm and bless, in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit.
  R. Amen.

EXHORTATION TO THE FAITHFUL TO PRAY

The priest addresses those present: 

  I take all here present to witness that you have been joined in holy Matrimony, in the sight of God and His Church.

  Let us pray, brethren, for God's blessing upon the newlyweds, that they may faithfully fulfill what they have vowed.

Psalm 127

Blessed are all who fear the Lord, who walk in obedience to him.
 For you will eat the fruit of your labor, blessings and prosperity will be yours.
 Thy wife shall be as a fruitful vine on the sides of thy house.
 Thy children as olive plants round about thy table.
 Behold, thus shall the man be blessed who fears the Lord!
 May the Lord bless you from Zion, that you may see the prosperity of Jerusalem all the days of your life.
 May you see your children’s children: peace be upon Israel!
 Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.
 As it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end.
 Amen.
